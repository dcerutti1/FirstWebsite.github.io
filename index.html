About My lucy

My Lucy is a small dog with a big personality. She loves treats, dressing up, snuggling, and jumping in mud puddles. But Lucy is more than just a dog. She's my best friend, a cherished member of the family! Meet Lucy in this sweet, rhyming book, a book that will resonate with all dog lovers, young and old alike!
